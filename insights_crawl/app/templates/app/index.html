Facts

They survey data is interpreted to derive facts and norms. A Fact expresses a survey Q&A as a percentile; the response of the respondents to this Q&A compared to the total number of respondents. In case of a boolean response (no/yes), the response value is a 0 or 1. For a nominal response, the response value is normalized between 0 and 1. These Facts can be computed for the whole population, but it is also possible to compute these Facts for sub-groups of respondents. These sub-groups corresponds to Facets that characterize respondents such as Age group, Gender, Country. Having different Fact values for these sub-groups results into Norms. What are the average Fact values for the different groups and where do you see patterns or correlations.
